$ fixed / % pct rules carve a specific amount or percentage of each paycheck off to a bucket. Fund this bucket rules don't take a fixed cut — each one simply claims whatever's left after every rule above it, like an envelope catching the remainder. Order matters: list your fixed/percentage rules first, then a "fund this bucket" rule last to absorb whatever's left over. External rules mark money transferred out (shown in forecast). Rules fire automatically when a paycheck income transaction is entered — and the same rules also drive the Distribute suggestions for spreading unassigned money across your buckets.
